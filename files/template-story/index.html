Ring of Fire: Quakes Across the Globe

The "ring of fire" is an area skirting the Pacific Ocean that is associated with an almost continuous series of oceanic trenches, volcanic arcs, and volcanic belts and/or plate movements.

You can see some of the most epic geological phenomena in San Francisco and its Pacific environs via the USGS maps; they release data about Bay Area earthquake probabilities continuously; this interactive will work with these data to create a series of visualizations related to the earthquake tracking.
